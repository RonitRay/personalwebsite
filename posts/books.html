What is this page?

I'm going to use this page as a stand-in for my goodreads profile, because I'd like to have all my data in one place, and I'm also not really fond of the idea of doing Amazon's work for free. Mac Wright's 2017 post is an inspiration as well.

These are some books I've read in the last 6-8 years, along with my rating at the time of finishing each one. Some of them might show up as links if I add a review or copy one over from my Goodreads profile.

I intend to keep this updated as I go.
